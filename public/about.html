About Arihant

Arihant Jaiswal is a dedicated and dynamic individual whose journey from Gorakhpur to Bangalore exemplifies both ambition and adaptability. With a robust background in Computer Science and practical experience gained at a global leader like Amazon, Arihant stands out as a professional committed to growth and excellence.

Born and raised in Gorakhpur, a city known for its rich cultural heritage and historical significance, Arihant Jaiswal nurtured a passion for technology from a young age. This interest led him to pursue a Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) in Computer Science, where he honed his skills and developed a strong foundation in the field. His academic achievements laid the groundwork for a successful career in technology, demonstrating his commitment to understanding and innovating within the digital landscape.

In 2021, Arihant took a significant step in his professional journey by joining Amazon, a company renowned for its innovation and customer-centric approach. He began his career as a Customer Service Representative (CRC Associate), where he quickly adapted to the fast-paced and demanding environment of one of the world’s largest e-commerce platforms. His role involved addressing customer concerns, resolving issues, and ensuring a seamless experience for users. This position provided him with invaluable insights into customer needs and the operational intricacies of a leading global enterprise.

Arihant’s performance and dedication did not go unnoticed. After a year in the CRC role, he transitioned to the Delivery Station (DLS) team, a move that marked a significant shift in his career trajectory. The DLS team, integral to Amazon’s logistics and supply chain operations, presented Arihant with new challenges and opportunities. In this role, he was responsible for overseeing the efficiency of delivery processes, managing logistics, and ensuring that the high standards of service that Amazon is known for were consistently met. His ability to handle complex logistical tasks and work collaboratively with diverse teams demonstrated his adaptability and problem-solving skills.
